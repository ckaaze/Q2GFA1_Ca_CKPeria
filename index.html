Bahaghari: Love has no labels
Since 2013, the national alliance of LGBT+ groups, formations, and advocates in the Philippines has been known as Bahaghari. Among its noteworthy projects is Bahaghari's concentration on HIV testing and media exposure of SOGIE concerns. Bahaghari is an anti-heteropatriarchal, anti-feudal, anti-colonial, and anti-imperialist group that extends beyond the LGBT community. Bahaghari is an organization rooted in these militant traditions, and it is fighting for social reform in the Philippines alongside other marginalized groups.
